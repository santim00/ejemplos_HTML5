What are we doing here at Doppler to prevent spam and email abuse?

We have an excellent customer support team analyzing the origin of every List’s Subscribers of every active Doppler account. Users will not be
able to send any Campaigns until all of their Subscribers get checked out by multiple evaluations and check criteria.

Every new Doppler user must agree to our Terms of Use, Privacy Policy and Anti Spam Policy before they can start enjoying their Doppler account. If they refuse to accept these terms they won’t be able to login and use their accounts.

We offer ultra-customizable forms with simple or double opt-in for those users that are starting with Email Marketing and have no Subscribers List to import to their account.

We automatically insert an unsubscribe link in every campaign sent from our app so your subscribers can opt-out easily anytime they want to.

All of our Lists are automatically cleaned regularly of any soft, hard or undeliverable email bounces. All users will be able to set up this clean up from every 1 to 5 attempts.

How we deal with spam and email abuse here at Doppler?

We embed every email campaign sent from our servers with a Campaign ID, so recipients can easily report to us about any kind of spam or email abuse. When we get complaints through our abuse form, we will investigate immediately. If, after a detailed analysis the account appears suspicious or fraudulent, we’ll suspend or even delete this account if warranted.

Here at Doppler we also constantly evaluate all campaigns that are sent out by all of our users. We verify continuously that each of our users always adhere to all of our policies here at Doppler and we have an effective system in place for email analysis and SPAM detection.

What are we doing at Doppler to educate our users about spam and email abuse?

We started by creating a free online training program for all of our users called Doppler Academy. Implemented in 2009, we’ve been training our users about Email Marketing 101 and online marketing best practices. Our main topics include “how to get more leads, avoiding spam and gaining more subscribers.” Read about that here at doppleracademy.com

We also offer users a complete user guide about different topics such as: HTML Emails Guide, Lead generation Guide and Email Marketing. This guide is called: “The ABC’s for creating effective campaigns.”

We host periodical face-to-face meetings for our new users. These meetings are called Doppler Email Marketing Cycles.

Throughout Doppler’s user interface we also provide our users with links and background information to help them understand proper email etiquette and spam laws. Find them here at help.fromdoppler.com
