Email Marketing Campaign

The Email Marketing Campaign enables our clients to send a campaign to one or several mailing lists using a HTML or an email template. This template can be designed by our clients using all kinds of images to create their own look and feel. Links can also be included in the Campaign to obtain link tracking reports while the campaign is being monitored.

Bilingual Campaigns

The potential of multilingual messaging has never been greater. With the explosive growth of the Hispanic market, email campaigns in both English and Spanish version are a must. Doppler is not limited just to English or Spanish languages, though. Users can easily send and report on their Campaigns in the most frequently used languages in the world. With Doppler, your clients can select the appropriate language for receiving their Campaigns, providing you with the most effective business approach.

Word of Mouth Campaigns

The Word of Mouth Campaign enables our clients to send a campaign to one or several mailing lists just as the Email Marketing Campaign does. The difference is that the recipients of the Word of Mouth Campaign can then refer this campaign to a certain number of friends. This is easily done by clicking on a link at the bottom of the campaign they receive. Doppler then tracks the referring activity.

Social Media Integration

Today, social networks such as Facebook, Twitter or LinkedIn are used by millions of users for sharing feelings, ideas, thoughts and opportunities. Doppler allows you to integrate your Email Marketing Campaigns to them in order to boost your Campaigns viral potential.
