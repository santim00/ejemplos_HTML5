How does Doppler’s Client Manager works?

At Doppler we understand our clients need to measure the results of direct response campaigns. To meet this need, we developed "Client Manager", a simple and flexible solution that handles the administration of all types of email blasts and tracks all the information.

• Management of Email Campaigns

• Strategic planning of Emails

• Email Tracking and follow up

• Reports and analysis
