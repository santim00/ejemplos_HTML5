Wow! Features

Look no further - Email Marketing has never been so easy. Discover how Doppler's features can help you improve your Email Campaigns.

Manage your Subscribers

With Doppler you will be able to organize and refine your mailing lists flawlessly. This will let you reach your target market with a much more customized and effective message.

Powerful Drag&Drop Editor

In Doppler you will find the best text edition tools (WYSIWYG) and powerful templates to create the most awe-inspiring and effective Email Marketing campaigns.

Campaigns for Every Need

Doppler offers you 3 alternatives so you can choose the Email Marketing campaign that better fits your needs: Classic Campaign, Social Campaign & A/B Test Campaign.

Doppler allows you to measure your campaign's performance with fully-detailed statistical reports. By using them you will discover your strengths and weaknesses, how many people clicked on your links and even the demographic profile of your subscribers.
