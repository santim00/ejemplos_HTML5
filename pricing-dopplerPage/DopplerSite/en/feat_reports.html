Handy Real Time Reports

As effectiveness lies on the analysis of results, we have developed a powerful reports application that will allow you to track your Email Marketing campaign in real time.

Measure your Campaign's performance with Doppler Reports.

IP Tracking Report

Being able to access accurate information about our clients and prospects becomes crucial for defining market segments and reaching strategic decision-making. IP Tracking Report is a powerful tool that will provide you with access to highly relevant information about your subscribers; including: geographic location of the addressees at the time the email is opened, their browser and operating system. Doppler empowers you with a deeper knowledge of your clients and prospects for a better satisfaction of their needs, by optimizing your campaigns segmentation.

Daily Opens and Clicks

Having exact data about our customers and prospects is essential for segmentation and to make strategic decisions. IP Tracking is a valuable report that will give you access to highly relevant information about the people that read your emails.

Subscribers' Behavior Record

Getting to know your subscribers allows you to optimize your Email Marketing Campaigns. Manage your subscribers according to their behavior, remove them from a list or develop new strategies for them.

Click-Forward Report

The Click-Forward Report tracks the natural viral activity of your Email Marketing Campaigns. This report you will provide information about the emails of your Email Marketing Campaign your subscribers resent.

Removal Report

Is a metric that measures which subscribers decided to remove their contact from the lists during a particular campaign.

Link Report

The "Link Tracking" Report show the number of clicks each link in our Newsletter has. Also, lets you see who make click on them

Deliver and Opening Report

Discover how many emails reached destination, how many were opened or not and who chose to be removed from your lists. Besides, you will get reports that includes opening days with detailed graphics about the activity of the clicks in your campaigns. Export the information to Excel. Rebound Reports, Hard and Soft Bounced integrated Doppler generates a report of bounced emails from every sent campaign. Bounced emails are classified into two types: Soft bounce: A soft bounce is an email that cannot reach the recipient because of a temporary problem such as "full mailbox". The recipient's server exists, recognizes the email address as one from the list of users and tries to send it. However, if the server finds an obstacle, the email is returned. This email can be sent later to reach its target. Hard bounce: This type of bounce is permanent. It occurs when the server or mailbox do not exist. If we keep sending this email, the result will be the same. This report is very useful because it will allow us to clean the contact lists by deleting invalid email addresses.

Comparative Campaigns

You can select several Email Marketing Campaigns to create a Campaign Comparative Report. The report compares the Email Marketing Campaigns you selected on the basis of different rates such as opening rate and bouncing rates among others. These comparisons are useful for the optimization of future campaigns. All information can be exported to Excel or PDF for better visibility.
