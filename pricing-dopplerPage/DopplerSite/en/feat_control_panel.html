Bounce Management

When sending Email Marketing Campaigns, certain addresses bounce due to different reasons (full mailbox, invalid recipient address, etc.). With the Bounce Management Tool, those email addresses identified as bounced (Hard or Soft), get deactivated automatically once they reach the maximum amount of bounces outlined.
