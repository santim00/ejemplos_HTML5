Mailing Lists

By using Doppler, you will be able to manage your subscribers' lists in an independent way, customize fields and create filters.

Manage and optimize your list of subscribers

Master Subscribers

Keeping Mailing Lists clean and updated is one of the basic Email Marketing practices to optimize deliverability. Master Subscribers is a unique tool that enables you to manage your contacts in a flexible and dynamic way. It is useful for deleting duplicated contacts in databases and moving subscribers from one list to another, among other functions.
Take advantage of Master Subscribers and produce better results by targeting your subscribers' interests.

Bounce Management

Our Bounce Management tool allows you to keep track of bounced addresses in your Email Marketing Campaign. Our tool detects both hard and soft bounces and automatically removes those addresses from your list once they have reached a certain number of unsuccessful delivery attempts. Doppler makes Email Marketing easy for you! Accounts are initially configured with 2 hard bounces and 3 soft bounces. Through your easy - to – use Control Panel, you can customize your bounce management preferences.

Double Opt-in Forms

Doppler offers the possibility of updating your subscribers' lists directly from your own website. Copy and paste the automatically created code Doppler provides to set up a subscription form to add to your lists.

Filtered Lists

These types of lists will update themselves automatically according to filtering criteria such as gender, country, nationality; whichever information you had uploaded with your subscribers' emails. You can also filter your lists according to your campaigns results such as opened, not opened bounce, etc.
